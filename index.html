Hi! I'm Annie Kiyonaga, a writer and educator based in Queens, NY. I'm interested in a lot of things: literacy and education; books and publishing; art historical and cultural commentary.

I've worked for nonprofits, research labs, and schools as a teacher, curriculum designer, editor, writer, and research assistant. I have a master's degree in education from Harvard's Graduate School of Education, where I studied Language and Literacy. I was also a Morehead-Cain scholar at UNC-Chapel Hill, where I studied English and art history.

You can find me on Substack, or contact me at annakiyonaga@gmail.com.
